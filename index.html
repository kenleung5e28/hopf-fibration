Hopf Fibration

S^3 is represented as pairs of complex numbers (z1, z2) with |z1|^2 + |z2|^2 = 1.

The Hopf map sends each point of S^3 to a point on S^2: (2 Re(z1 conjugate(z2)), 2 Im(z1 conjugate(z2)), |z1|^2 - |z2|^2).

A fiber is all points (e^(it) z1, e^(it) z2) for a fixed base point on S^2. Each rendered curve samples one such fiber.

Stereographic projection converts points from S^3 in R^4 to visible curves in R^3. This app projects from the +d pole.
